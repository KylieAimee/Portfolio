Art Journey

I have enjoyed creating art since I was a kid. I have used many different mediums, mainly tradition and some of my favourites are:

  1. Graphite: I love how easily you can work with it, and how realistic images can get with it, though it can be hard to get those dark blacks

  2. Charcoal: I love how even more realistic and dark you can get your images, although it can get very messy

  3. Watercolour: I love how it looks and how easily art can be created with it, especially more abstract art, although mistakes can be made very easily

  4. Pencil Crayon: I love how you can blend them and the soft images that can be created with them

  5. Digital: I love how easily things can be deleted or undone without leaving marks, and how perfessional it can look, though it can be a big learning curve

Currently I am learning Digital art for graphic design.
